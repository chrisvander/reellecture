About

We classify and aggregate engagement and emotions to create a more positive online learning environment

  • 01 For Teachers

    At the end of a lecture, we'll give you a report on the overall attentiveness and engagement of your students. We also provide real-time alerts when the majority of the class is confused! It's perfect to help read the room while being in virtual settings!

  • 02 For Students

    Don't worry, our goal was not to let teachers spy on you. We're students too, and we know how easy it is to pick up your phone during a lecture and scroll through Tik Tok. We'll alert you to help you get back on track and ultimately help you focus during school so you can have more time in your day.

  • 03 For Parents

    You no longer have to be watching over your child's shoulder as they attend class online! You can sign up to receive to daily or weekly updates about your child's engagement in classes.
